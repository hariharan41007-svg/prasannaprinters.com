PRASANNA PRINTERS & DIGITALS

Since its inception in 2005, Prasanna Printers & Digitals has stood as a titan in the printing industry of Old Washermenpet, Chennai. We are more than just a printing shop; we are a high-capacity industrial manufacturing hub.

Our facility specializes in utilizing heavy-duty technology to serve corporate, industrial, and creative sectors. We combine 20+ years of legacy with modern digital sophistication to deliver results that are sharp, durable, and visually commanding.

Industrial Powerhouse

Global Standard Machinery

Konica Minolta Digital Production Press

The crown jewel of our digital department. This industrial-grade Konica Minolta press is designed for high-throughput production, capable of maintaining 100% color accuracy across massive print runs. With a stunning 1200 dpi resolution, it delivers photographic quality that makes marketing brochures, business cards, and catalogs look vibrant and professional. It is the perfect engine for clients who demand the speed of digital with the heavy-duty quality of offset printing.

Industrial HP LaserJet Xerox Station

Engineered for relentless reliability. Our HP industrial xerox station is the workhorse for high-volume document management. It handles thousands of pages per hour, producing crisp, sharp black-and-white text and graphics. Ideal for corporate manuals, legal documents, and educational bulk orders, this machine ensures that even the largest "mass" production tasks are completed with razor-sharp clarity and professional sorting in record time.

AB Deck Offset Printing Machine

The powerhouse of traditional printing. Our AB Deck offset machine is specialized for heavy cardstock and high-volume specialty jobs. It provides a tactile, premium feel that digital machines cannot achieve, making it the top choice for luxury wedding invitations, official certificates, and industrial packaging. By utilizing high-torque mechanical systems, it delivers deep ink penetration and consistent results for massive batches that require a permanent, high-authority look.

Hydraulic Programmable Paper Cutter

Precision finishing at an industrial scale. No project is complete without the perfect edge. Our programmable hydraulic cutter uses massive pressure and a razor-sharp blade to slice through hundreds of sheets with mathematical accuracy (down to 0.1mm). This ensures that every single flyer, card, or brochure in your order is perfectly squared and factory-clean, providing the final touch of professional excellence to your brand’s physical materials.
